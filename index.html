Our Story

Fashion for Every Occasion

Rithika's Fashion is your trusted neighbourhood boutique in Mettu Thandalam, Thiruporur. We bring you the latest trends in ethnic and western wear — from elegant sarees and churidars to stylish kurtis, kids wear and exclusive festival collections, all under one roof.

Located on Kannathasan Street, opposite the Boys Government Higher Secondary School, our store is conveniently accessible to customers from Thandalam, Thiruporur and the surrounding areas. We are committed to offering quality fashion at affordable prices, making every shopping experience a joyful one for you and your family.
